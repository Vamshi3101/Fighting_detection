Fighting Detection

About Project

A violence detection model is an artificial intelligence system designed to recognize and identify instances of violence or aggression in various forms of media, such as images, videos, or audio recordings. The model uses machine learning algorithms to analyze visual and auditory cues, including body language, facial expressions, tone of voice, and contextual information, to determine whether a particular piece of content contains violent or aggressive behavior. The ultimate goal of a violence detection model is to provide an automated way to monitor potentially harmful content and alert relevant authorities, such as law enforcement agencies or social media platforms, to take appropriate action.
